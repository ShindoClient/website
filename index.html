What is ShindoClient?

We are a free Minecraft 1.8.9 PVP client with a unqiue modern design that is easy to use.

ShindoClient is a enchanced fork of SoarClient with various improvements including fixing bugs, more mods, better performance and more!

We have unique mods you wont find elsewhere and customisability that is unrivialed.

We started ShindoClient originally as a SoarClient 7 update however after SoarClient got discontinued by EldoDebug the 2 developers working on that update seperated it and made ShindoClient so people could keep experiencing our modern design.

We hope you will join our community and experience the client of the future!
